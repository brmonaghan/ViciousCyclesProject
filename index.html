What is Vicious Cycles?

Vicious Cycles is a state-owned bike store that is located halfway between Fayetteville and Raleigh in North Carolina. We sell a wide variety of manual and automatic bicycles, including mopeds, bicycles, tricycles, electronic bikes, hoverboards and much more. We also sell motorcycles from the top names of the business including Harley Davidsons, Hondas, Suzukis, and Kawasakis, to name a few. For manual bicycles we carry such lines as Schwinn, Huffy, and so much more. In addition, we even have accessories, and purchase your old bicycle.

About Us

Vicious Cycles, Inc. is an Erwin, North Carolina-based cycle workshop founded by John Doe in late 1998.

Our customer service staff attends to the day-to-day operations, even with the ongoing pandemic! We believe that each bike should represent the personality, and lifestyle of its owner.

We believe in the significance of individualism.

We believe in having fun, but safely.

We believe Vicious Cycles, Inc. will help make your dreams come true.
