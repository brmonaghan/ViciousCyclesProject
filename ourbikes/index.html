How to Buy Our Bicycles

Vicious Cycles products can be purchased/ordered at our store, located on 143 Main Street in Erwin NC.

Due to the pandemic, store hours have been adjusted because of cleaning efforts.

You can also make an appointment on our website to speak to one of our representatives in person.

Masks are required to be worn if coming into our store.

All custom builds will need a 25% down payment before projects will begin and must be paid in full, when completed. Custom bikes can be returned within six months of purchase with a receipt.
