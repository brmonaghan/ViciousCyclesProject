How Do We Build Bikes?

Due to the COVID pandemic, our production cycle is lasting approximately two-four weeks depending on the style, make and model.

All materials are ordered by an affiliated company. The materials are then shipped directly to our store to begin the production process.

We will notify a customer immediately if there is a delay in production.

Our hours of operation are from 8-5 Monday through Friday, pickups and deliveries are typically scheduled on Saturday mornings. We are closed on Sundays.
